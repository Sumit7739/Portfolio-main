Invenio - Overview

Invenio is a clean and powerful web-based inventory and sales management system tailored for small to mid-sized businesses. It allows administrators to track stock, manage sales, and generate real-time reports from a centralized dashboard.

The system includes modules for product management, sales processing, customer tracking, and detailed reporting‚Äî all designed to streamline daily operations and support data-driven decision-making.

With a user-friendly interface and robust backend logic, Invenio empowers businesses to stay organized, reduce manual work, and maintain inventory accuracy without complexity.

Duration: 6 weeks Client: ****** Tools: HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, MySQL
The project aimed to create a reliable, scalable, and intuitive system to manage inventory and sales workflows effectively.

Key Features

A comprehensive suite of tools that support operational efficiency and accuracy in inventory management.

Dynamic Dashboard

A responsive dashboard providing real-time summaries of sales, inventory status, and recent activities.

  • Live Inventory Snapshot: Always stay updated with current stock levels.
  • Sales Tracking: Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly transactions at a glance.
  • Alerts & Notifications: Get notified when inventory drops below thresholds.

Inventory Management

A powerful module to manage product stock, categories, and suppliers with ease.

  • Product CRUD Operations: Add, edit, or delete products and categorize them efficiently.
  • Supplier Details: Manage vendor information and restock logs.
  • Stock Adjustment Logs: Every change is recorded for transparency.

Sales Processing & Reporting

Built-in tools to handle transactions and analyze sales performance over time.

  • Easy Sale Entry: Add new sales with just a few clicks.
  • Customer Info: Store customer details and sales history.
  • Custom Reports: Filter sales by date, product, or customer for granular insights.
